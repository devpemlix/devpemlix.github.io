SUMMARY

If you want to make your home more comfortable, energy-efficient and eco-friendly, one of the best things you can do is to insulate your loft. Loft insulation is a simple and cost-effective way to prevent heat loss through your roof, which can account for up to 25% of your heating bills.

FEATURES

Loft insulation works by creating a barrier between the inside and outside of your roof, trapping the warm air inside and keeping the cold air out. This means you will need less energy to heat your home in winter and cool it in summer, saving you money and reducing your carbon emissions.
