Solar

Solar panels are another great way to save money and energy, as they allow you to produce your own renewable electricity from the sun. By installing solar panels, you can save up to £240 per year on your electricity bills, according to the Solar Trade Association. You can also earn money by selling any excess electricity back to the grid through the Smart Export Guarantee scheme. Plus, you can reduce your CO2 emissions by up to 1.3 tonnes per year.

Loft insulation

Loft insulation is one of the most effective ways to keep your home warm and cosy, as it prevents heat from escaping through the roof. By insulating your loft, you can save up to £250 per year on your energy bills, according to the Energy Saving Trust. You can also reduce your CO2 emissions by up to 1 tonne per year
