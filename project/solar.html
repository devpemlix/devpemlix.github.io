SUMMARY

Solar energy is one of the most abundant, clean and renewable sources of power in the world. It can help you reduce your carbon footprint, lower your energy bills and increase your energy independence. But how does it work and what are the benefits of going solar?

FEATURES

Solar energy works by converting sunlight into electricity using photovoltaic (PV) panels. These panels can be installed on your roof, ground or other suitable locations, depending on your space and preference. The electricity generated by the panels can be used to power your appliances, lights and devices, or stored in batteries for later use. You can also sell any excess electricity back to the grid and earn credits or money.
