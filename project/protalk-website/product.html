Welcome To Protalk

A dialer is a software program that automates the process of making and receiving phone calls, usually for sales, marketing, or customer service purposes. A login page is the screen that a user sees when they launch a dialer application, and it should provide a secure and convenient way of entering their username and password.

Realtime Call Flow

The real-time call flow of a dialer is the sequence of events that occurs when a dialer application initiates and handles phone calls, usually for sales, marketing, or customer service purposes. A dialer can operate in different modes, such as power dialer, predictive dialer, or progressive dialer, depending on the desired speed and efficiency of the calls

Realtime Agent Details

The dialer application connects to a database of contacts and selects a number of leads to call based on certain criteria, such as location, availability, or priority. The dialer application places the calls using a voice over IP (VoIP) service or a phone system, and monitors the status of each call, such as ringing, busy, no answer, or answered.
